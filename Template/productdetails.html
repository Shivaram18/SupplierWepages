Tglia California Almonds

23 almonds a day - is a phrase often used in California and parts of the United States to convey that 23 almonds are enough for good health. California's almonds are grown in the most natural ecosystem using crop protection, dust reduction and pollination tools that are certified in terms of hygiene and safety. As a result, California Almonds are of supreme quality and are used as a healthy snack across the globe. We have sourced our California Almonds from the original farms of the State of California, which is known for its almond cultivation. Due to the utmost care taken during their growing and harvesting season, California Almonds retain essential nutrients and health benefits.
